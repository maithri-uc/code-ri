26-5-1 — 26-5-17. Repealed.

Repealed Sections.

These sections ( P.L. 1907 , ch. 1470, §§ 1-6; G.L. 1909 , ch. 103, §§ 1-6; P.L. 1916 , ch. 1381, § 1; G.L. 1923 , ch. 115, §§ 1-6; G.L. 1923 , ch. 115, §§ 7-9, as enacted by P.L. 1929 , ch. 1426, §§ 1, 2; G.L. 1938 , ch. 65, §§ 1-9; P.L. 1939 , ch. 660, § 96, as enacted by P.L. 1954 , ch. 3344, § 1; P.L. 1944 , ch. 1441, § 36; G.L. 1956 , §§ 26-5-1 to 26-5-17; P.L. 1961 , ch. 135, § 1; P.L. 1962 , ch. 21, § 1) were repealed by P.L. 1967 , ch. 223, § 2, P.L. 1968 , ch. 178, § 2, and by P.L. 1970 , ch. 324, § 2.
